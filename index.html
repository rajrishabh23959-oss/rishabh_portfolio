About Me

I'm Rishabh Raj, currently pursuing Banking Technology & CSBS at Pondicherry University. My work sits at the intersection of two worlds that don't usually talk to each other — low-level engineering (data structures, algorithms, backend systems) and financial ecosystems (core banking, economics, fintech security).

Outside of coursework, I build things: a behavioral-biometrics security concept for banking authentication, a physics-driven 2D game engine, and DSA problem-solving practice on GitHub. I'm drawn to problems where clean logic and real-world trust — like how a bank verifies it's really you — meet.

I grew up in Begusarai, Bihar, and now split my time between home and campus in Puducherry. Being fluent across five languages has made cross-team, cross-region communication second nature — useful for a field where engineering and business have to speak the same language.
