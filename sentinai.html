How It Works
⌨️

Capture

Every keydown & keyup event is timestamped at millisecond precision during normal login — zero extra steps for the user.

🧠

Analyze

Dwell time, flight time, rhythm entropy, and burst patterns are extracted as a unique behavioral fingerprint per session.

🛡️

Decide

Isolation Forest model compares live session against enrolled baseline. Anomalies trigger step-up auth or instant block.
